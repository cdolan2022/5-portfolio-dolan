Who I am

My name is Claire Dolan I was born in Milwaukee Wisconsin on November 27 2003. I moved to a new town in 2007 and attended a private school there until 6th grade, when I switched to the public school system. I am currently a student in high school. I have a small family and love my friends. I love to play with my cat and ride my bike places. I travel alot and have been to places like Iceland, New Zealand, and Ireland. goals and causes

I care a lot about the environment, nature, and saving the planet. I think it's super important to keep our earth clean and safe. I always advocate for the planet whether I’m at school or at home. I believe we need to lessen our carbon footprint, use wind and solar energy sources, and stop littering and wasting. I am working on getting the school to use biodegradable straws along with 2 friends. I always thought that I needed to go somewhere and do something big to help my cause but I realize now that I can make a change right here at school. I try not to let people tell me I can't do something or that I shouldn’t be offended by litter. It is my goal to educate people on the dangers of, litter and global warming. I also want to go to a good college and study Marine Biology. I hope to get married and have a good job studying the ocean or rescuing animals.
